Rules of the competition

Phase 1

Maximum number of participants 5 and minimum number of participant 2.
A maximum dimension of hovercraft is not exceeding 30cmX30cmX35cm.
Teams must bring their own tools.
Round 1: Idea Submission
Round 2: Submission of detailed abstract of the project
The top 30 scoring teams will advance to the next phase.

Phase 2

Throughout this phase, the top 30 teams will construct their projects and be continually assessed via project reports.
Participants will be able to seek guidance from a panel of experts.

Phase 3

On the event's finale, the teams must submit a video presentation of their concept and demonstrate a functional prototype to the judges.
